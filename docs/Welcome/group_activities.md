# Group Activities

Our research group meets once a week, each Thursday, for a **Journal Club (JC)** and a **Group Meeting (GM)**.

---

## Weekly Meetings

### Journal Club (JC)

The Journal Club is run between Birmingham and Oxford, focused on **Systems Neuroscience**. During the JC, one of us presents a paper they found interesting, and we discuss it together in-depth. This is a great space to share thoughts, practise presenting, and engage in friendly discussion.

- **When:** Every Thursday at 10 AM
- **Format:** In-person at OHBA, with [Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_NjU5OTUyMWItMTFhNy00M2U3LTliYjgtMzYxNWZjOTkyMTNk%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22cc95de1b-97f5-4f93-b4ba-fe68b852cf91%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%22d333db4b-be9c-40d5-b7f4-77e8eda37271%22%7d) attendance available

To present a paper at the JC, contact:
- Jenny Anisimova — [jenny.anisimova@psych.ox.ac.uk](mailto:jenny.anisimova@psych.ox.ac.uk)
- Jiaqi Li — [jiaqi.li@psych.ox.ac.uk](mailto:jiaqi.li@psych.ox.ac.uk)

### Group Meeting (GM)

Group Meetings are reserved for core members of the **Neuronal Oscillations group**. We usually invite an external speaker to present their research. Internal speakers are also welcome to use a GM slot to present their own work (e.g., to practise a grant proposal presentation).

- **When:** Every Thursday at 11 AM
- **Format:** In-person at OHBA, with [Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_NjU5OTUyMWItMTFhNy00M2U3LTliYjgtMzYxNWZjOTkyMTNk%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22cc95de1b-97f5-4f93-b4ba-fe68b852cf91%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%22d333db4b-be9c-40d5-b7f4-77e8eda37271%22%7d) attendance available

To suggest a speaker for the GM, contact:
- Jenny Anisimova — [jenny.anisimova@psych.ox.ac.uk](mailto:jenny.anisimova@psych.ox.ac.uk)
