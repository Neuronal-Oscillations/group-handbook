# HR-Related Information

### Annual Leave
Request via [HR Self-service](https://staff.admin.ox.ac.uk/working-at-oxford/you-and-work/hr-self-service) for approval by PI.

### Sickness
Notify PI and log via [HR Self-service](https://staff.admin.ox.ac.uk/working-at-oxford/you-and-work/hr-self-service).

### Personal Development Review (PDR)
Probation reviews and yearly PDRs are conducted with the PI and documented on [Simitive](https://psychiatrypdr.simitive.com/).

### Personal Profile
Group members should keep their personal profile on the Oxford website up to date — see an [example here](https://www.psy.ox.ac.uk/people/ole-jensen). To update your profile, navigate to your page and log in at the upper right corner.

### ORCID
Group members should update their ORCID with their Oxford University affiliation. See [this guidance](https://orcid.web.ox.ac.uk/home) for more information.
